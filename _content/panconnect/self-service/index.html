What is PanConnect Self-Service?

PanConnect Self-Service brings an integrated web & mobile experience directly to your end customers. It enables them to access their account information, pay bills, check statements, open issues, access and fill out forms, access information and documents, discover upcoming events in the local area, and much more.

The product is stylable, to a high degree, so you can make it suit your corporate colour palette and branding.

Offering a digital self-service portal is in many customers' eyes an essential part of delivering a quality customer experience. PanConnect Self-Service will ensure you deliver that experience.

Technical

Offline working, by design

A mobile-optimised copy of your master data is stored on the mobile device. All app configuration is stored locally too. Provides high performance and seamless working in bad signal areas.

Unrivalled synchronisation technology

Our app automatically selects from several different kinds of sync for various different types of data and scenarios, all of which are some combination of a “incremental” and/or “binary differential” protocol. All sync data is compressed with a “LZMA” algorithm to ensure the absolute best bandwidth savings. Our best engineers worked on this technology to ensure your monthly mobile data plan bills are kept as low as possible.

Native app experience

A true native app experience that offers the best performance, look & feel and able to take advantage of the latest hardware platform features.

Integration

PanConnect can pull your master data from almost any source, including:
