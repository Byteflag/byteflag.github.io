What is PanConnect Self-Service?

PanConnect Self-Service brings an integrated web & mobile experience directly to your end customers. It enables them to access their account information, pay bills, check statements, open issues, access and fill out forms, access information and documents, discover upcoming events in the local area, and much more.

The product has first class re-styling capabilities, so you can make it suit your corporate colour palette and branding. The mobile apps' name, icon, colour scheme and other graphics can also be rebranded. You will have your own branded set of app store links on iTunes, Google Play Store and Windows Store.

Offering a digital self-service portal is in many end customers' eyes an essential part of delivering a quality customer experience. PanConnect Self-Service will ensure you deliver that experience.

Integration

Data sources

PanConnect can pull your master data from almost any source, including:

  • ODBC-compatible databases like MSSQL, SQLite, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Progress, Ingres, and hundreds more.
  • XML, JSON, CSV files
  • Excel spreadsheets & Access databases
  • REST and SOAP web services
  • E-mail
  • PDF files including “contiguous print runs” with intelligent pattern-matching page splitting.

Special integrations

We've created some bespoke integration modules for certain third-party systems and markets to save you time, including:

  • Social Housing databases: Capita, Civica, Northgate, Orchard and many more.
  • Electronic document stores: Capita Swordfish, Azure DocumentDB, Amazon Web Services, Sharepoint and more.
  • Our MacroPilot tool, a screen oriented macro engine, that can replicate the actions of a human; for those trickiest of systems that lack any integration points.

Encrypted, efficient, fault tolerant

Our integration toolset was designed from the outset to be highly secure, efficient and reliable.

All communications between your on-premise servers and our servers (if you host with us) are conducted across a highly reliable messaging service bus that is 2048-bit SSL secured. Everything is also highly compressed to save bandwidth.

The PanConnect platform is enterprise ready.
