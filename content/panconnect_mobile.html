Solas Cymru May 2015

Solas, a supported people social housing association with operations in Wales (UK) and owned by Seren Group, has begun deployment of PanConnect Mobile and Self-Service, delivered by Housing Insight.

Seren Group May 2015

Seren Group, a large group of social housing associations with operations in Wales (UK) and which emcompasses Solas Cymru, has agreed an option with Housing Insight to deploy the PanConnect product suite pending delivery of Solas Cymru.

RCT Homes March 2015

RCT Homes, a social housing association with operations in Wales (UK), has reached "live status" with our PanConnect Mobile solution.

In May 2015, RCT Homes agreed three additional phases of development extending well into 2015.

United Welsh March 2015

United Welsh has reached "live status" with our PanConnect Self-Service solution, enabling their customers with digital access to their tenancy account through web portal and mobile apps.
