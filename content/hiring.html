Since our team is currently comprised of distributed, you would need to be okay with that. Though our central base in London and that's where any formal meetings or social events would happen.

We realise that often the biggest drawback of distributed teams is being stuck working from home. Not with us. Providing you live in a city we can almost certainly get you a shared office space to work from.
